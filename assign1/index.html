OUR STORY

Foodaholic has been established in Jagtial in the year 2009, and since then we are continuously spreading our wings to give our customers a real Chinese spicy dining and takeaway experience. 

The freshness of our products when combined with our homemade spices, ginger garlic paste, and sauces are made which provide a unique dining experience that is exclusive to Foodaholic.

We began by offering standard Chinese food for the first time in Jagtial, and today Foodaholic has become a synonym for mouth watering manchurian to tempting dum biryani!!!
